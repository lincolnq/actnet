# actnet

A social network for activism organizing. The core idea: people install the app because a specific action (a rally, a canvass, a strike) requires it, and stay because the network captures the real social connections they formed through that participation.

The design centers on Signal-quality encrypted messaging — a unified inbox of all your conversations across all your activism — with a platform layer ("Projects") on top for building organizing tools: team assignment, action-day maps, Q&A bots, collaborative documents, and more.
